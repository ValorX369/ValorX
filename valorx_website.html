About ValorX

ValorX (VLX) is a simple value-holding token with a structured burn mechanism. Starting with a supply of 250 million, 50 million VLX will be burned over the next year, reducing total supply and increasing scarcity.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 250,000,000 VLX

Burn Mechanism: 50,000,000 VLX over one year

Why ValorX?

  • Deflationary supply ensures long-term value
  • Simple and transparent economic model
  • Reliable and fair distribution
